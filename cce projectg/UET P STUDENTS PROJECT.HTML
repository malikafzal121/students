History & Importance of UET Peshawar

Established in 1952, UET Peshawar began as the University College of Engineering under the University of Peshawar. In 1980, it became an independent university. It has grown to become the leading engineering institution in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, supporting innovation, education, and research in science and engineering.

University Rankings

  • Top 10 engineering universities in Pakistan (HEC).
  • Recognized in QS World University Rankings.
  • Accredited by PEC (Pakistan Engineering Council).
  • Ranked 3rd in Pakistan by number of completion of courses and enrollments in April 2025.

Admission Information

Admissions open every year after FSc/Intermediate exams. Entry Test (EATA or ECAT) is mandatory. Documents required:

  • FSc Marksheet
  • Domicile Certificate
  • CNIC or Form-B
  • Recent Passport Size Photos
  • Matric Marksheet
  • Etea Marksheet
  • Affidavit

Hostel Information

UET provides secure hostel accommodation for both male and female students. For newly enrolled mele students there are two hostles Hostle_3 Shah Waliullah Hostle Old block and Hostle 12 Nishtar hostle. Both are good depending on seasons. Hostle 3 room are normally cooler then hostle 12 for summer also in winter hostle 3 is warmer then hostle 12,therefore your first priority must be hostle 3. Facilities include:

  • Shared Rooms
  • Mess & Dining Hall
  • Wi-Fi & Electricity
  • Common Rooms, Study rooms, Indoor game & Security

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How can I apply?
A: Visit the admission office or apply online when admissions open.
Q: Are hostels available for all?
A: Seats are limited and allotted based on merit and availability.
Q: Is there any transport facility?
A: Yes, limited university buses operate on fixed routes like for girls hostle and far away departments.
Q: What to do if I miss orientation?
A: Contact your department and visit the Student Affairs office.
Q: Can I change my department after admission?
A: Department changes are subject to university policy and seat availability.
Q: What facilities are available for sports?
A: UET has cricket grounds, basketball courts, Futsal ground, Tennis courts and many more indoor sports facilities.
